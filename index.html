Meet your guide

Hi, I'm Patty Moore.

With over 25 years of experience teaching art, I create fun, relaxing, and inspiring painting experiences for individuals and groups of all levels.

Whether you've never painted before or already love to create, my sessions are designed to help you unwind, explore your creativity, and leave with your own original artwork. I guide each person at their own pace — helping everyone feel confident and inspired.
